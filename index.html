Welcome to the Santa's Devs Project

Dive into the festive spirit as you embark on a journey through our interactive greeting card experience. So, let the festivities begin, and may your creations bring smiles and holiday magic to all!

Music Player with Christmas Songs

1. Click on the "Music" tab to access the playlist.

2. Choose your favorite Christmas song from the list.

3. Press play and enjoy the festive tunes while creating your card.

Christmas Card Generator

Go to the "Card" tab. Type your heartfelt message on the virtual card. Use formatting options for a personalized touch.

Sending the Card:

1. After composing your message, click on the "Send" button.

2. Choose to share the card by generating a link or downloading it.

Share the link or send the downloaded card to your recipient to spread the holiday cheer!

Happy card-making and spreading joy!
