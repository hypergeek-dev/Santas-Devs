  • 1. Choose a design from the carousel. You can use the arrows or the box to choose a design
  • 2. Add a recipient, greeting message and sender.
  • 3. The design will preview in the window below the carousel.
  • 4. Once ready - click the shareable link so it can viewed in another page or download a copy to your device.
  • 5. Have fun!
